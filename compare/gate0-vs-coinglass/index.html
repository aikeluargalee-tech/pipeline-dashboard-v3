Gate0 vs CoinGlass

Free regime detection vs paid liquidation terminal

What CoinGlass Does Best

CoinGlass is the best liquidation heatmap tool available. It shows you exactly where leveraged positions are concentrated, across multiple timeframes and exchanges. If you want to see the raw liquidation landscape — every cluster, every magnet, every leverage level — CoinGlass is the tool.

Fun fact: Gate0 actually uses Coinglass data. The heatmap capture job scrapes Coinglass liquidation heatmaps and feeds them into our structural analysis layer.

What Gate0 Does Differently

CoinGlass shows you a heatmap. Gate0 tells you what it means.

Raw heatmap data is just an image — bright spots showing where liquidations will cascade. Gate0 takes that data and computes sandwich geometry: how far is price from the nearest above and below clusters? Are they compressed (Vice Grip)? Is one side much closer (Squeeze/Sweep)?

This turns a heatmap image into a tradeable regime label. Instead of staring at colors, you get: "Downside Sweep active — below magnet is 3x closer than above. Long liquidations likely."
