Gate0 vs Glassnode

Free multi-layer synthesis vs paid on-chain data platform

What Glassnode Does Best

Glassnode is the gold standard for on-chain data. If you want to track every Bitcoin UTXO, analyze holder cohorts by age, monitor exchange balances across dozens of exchanges, or build custom indicators from raw blockchain data — Glassnode is unmatched. Their charting library is extensive, their data is clean, and their indicators are well-documented.

For professional analysts who need to build their own interpretations from raw data, Glassnode is worth every penny.

What Gate0 Does Differently

Glassnode gives you charts. Gate0 gives you a unified model synthesis. The fundamental difference: synthesis vs data.

Glassnode shows you MVRV Z-Score, SOPR, and Puell Multiple as separate charts. You interpret them. Gate0 takes those same metrics, normalizes them into a 0-100 composite score, maps them to cycle phases (ACCUMULATION through CYCLE TOP), and weights them at 0.5x in the overall synthesis because monthly-scale data shouldn't outvote live structural events.

Glassnode doesn't tell you whether to trade. Gate0 does — with position-sizing parameters.

When to Use Which

Use Gate0 when: You want a clear risk synthesis on whether to trade, what size, and what risks are active — without interpreting 50 charts yourself.
Use Glassnode when: You're a professional analyst who needs raw on-chain data to build custom interpretations, or you need UTXO-level cohort analysis.

They're not mutually exclusive. Many professionals use Glassnode for deep on-chain research and Gate0 for the daily report and risk gating.
