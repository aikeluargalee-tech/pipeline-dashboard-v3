Gate0 vs CryptoQuant

Free multi-layer synthesis vs paid exchange flow analysis

What CryptoQuant Does Best

CryptoQuant excels at exchange flow analysis — tracking Bitcoin moving in and out of exchanges, miner outflows, stablecoin reserves, and inter-exchange flows. Their strength is showing you where coins are moving and who's moving them. For understanding the supply side of the equation, CryptoQuant is excellent.

What Gate0 Does Differently

CryptoQuant focuses on one dimension (exchange flows) in depth. Gate0 covers six dimensions (macro, structure, derivatives, on-chain, cycle, TA) and synthesizes them into one risk synthesis.

Gate0 does track exchange netflow as part of the cycle layer, but it's one input among many. The difference is that Gate0 asks "given all these inputs together, what should I do?" while CryptoQuant asks "what are the flows telling us?"
