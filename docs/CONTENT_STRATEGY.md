# Content Strategy — Pipeline Dashboard

## Positioning

**Pipeline Dashboard is the macro-first Bitcoin intelligence platform.** The differentiator is the inverted reading order: structure, liquidity, and positioning first; price last. Every piece of content reinforces that thesis.

**Voice:** analytical, honest, slightly contrarian, jargon-aware. Never hype. Never price predictions. Always "here's how to think," not "here's what to buy."

## Audience Tiers

1. **Curious newcomers** — found via "how to read the bitcoin market," "what is MVRV." Entry: glossary + beginner-friendly dashboard mode.
2. **Active traders** — found via metric-specific and framework queries. Entry: research articles, methodology, dashboard.
3. **AI answer engines** — ChatGPT/Perplexity/Google AI Overviews extracting definitions. Served by FAQ schema, glossary, and prose methodology.

## Content Principles

- **Original analysis over volume.** Depth beats thin AI-spun posts. Publish weekly, not daily.
- **Answer-first structure.** Lead with the direct answer, then expand — wins AI Overviews and featured snippets.
- **Entity density.** Name metrics, sources, and concepts precisely; link them internally.
- **Honesty as a moat.** Published failure modes and calibrated confidence are rare — lean into them.
- **Never give advice.** Educational framing + disclaimer on every page.
