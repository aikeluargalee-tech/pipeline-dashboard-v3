Gate0: A Multi-Layer Risk Framework for Bitcoin Trading

Pillar article · Risk Frameworks cluster · 12 min read

Most trading systems focus on when to enter. Gate0 focuses on whether you should be trading at all. It's a pre-trade risk filter that sits above every other analysis layer — and it can veto everything.

The Philosophy

Position sizing matters more than signal accuracy. A perfect entry signal is worthless if you're trading full size during a systemic risk event. Gate0 answers one question before any other analysis runs: is it safe to trade right now?

The Four States

  • 🟢 PROCEED — Full position size, normal rules. No systemic risk detected.
  • 🟡 TIGHTENED — Max 10x leverage, half position size, tight stops. Elevated risk — reduce exposure.
  • 🟠 PAUSE — Close existing positions only, no new entries. Significant risk — stand aside.
  • 🔴 ABORT — Flatten everything. Extreme systemic risk — do not open the app.

The Five Modules

1. Black Swan (0-17 Score)

A composite tail-risk score aggregating multiple systemic signals: VIX term inversion, DXY spikes, funding rate extremes, stablecoin depeg, exchange anomalies, and geopolitical events. Each signal contributes to a score from 0 to 17.

Thresholds: ≥ 12 → ABORT, ≥ 8 → PAUSE, ≥ 5 → TIGHTENED, < 5 → PROCEED.

2. VIX/SPX

Monitors equity market volatility and its spillover into Bitcoin. VIX > 40 signals panic that historically correlates with BTC sell-offs. The module also checks SPX/RSI divergence for early warning.

Thresholds: VIX > 40 → ABORT, > 30 → PAUSE, > 22 → TIGHTENED.

3. AI-3 Wave

An experimental module tracking hyperscaler capex and capex-to-revenue divergence — a proxy for the "AI bubble" thesis. When active, it signals elevated risk of a tech-led risk-off event that would impact BTC.

Threshold: Active → TIGHTENED.

4. Stablecoins

Monitors USDT and USDC for depeg events. Stablecoin instability is a leading indicator of crypto-specific systemic risk — if the dollars backing crypto trades are unstable, everything is unstable.

Thresholds: Deviation > 2% → ABORT, > 0.5% → PAUSE.

5. Session

Time-of-day and day-of-week liquidity regime. Weekend trading is thinner and signals are noisier. The session module automatically downgrades to TIGHTENED on weekends.

How the Verdict Is Computed

The most restrictive module wins. If any module says ABORT, the gate is ABORT — regardless of what other modules say. The state priority is:

ABORT (4) > PAUSE (3) > TIGHTENED (2) > PROCEED (1)

Trigger order matters. If Black Swan scores ≥ 4 or AI-3 wave flips active, the gate moves to TIGHTENED regardless of other modules. PAUSE/ABORT requires two or more modules in escalation.

The L-1 Manual Override

Some risks can't be detected by algorithms — wars, regulatory crackdowns, exchange hacks that haven't been announced yet. The L-1 Manual Macro Gate allows a human to override the entire system with a PAUSE or ABORT verdict. When active, it overrides all automated analysis.

The manual gate supports auto-expiry — you set a re-evaluation time and trigger condition, and the gate automatically deactivates when the condition is met or the expiry passes.

Gate0 in the Synthesis

Gate0 doesn't just produce a standalone verdict — it gates the entire synthesis:

  • If Gate0 = ABORT → synthesis = "DO NOT TRADE"
  • If Gate0 = PAUSE → synthesis = "STAND ASIDE"
  • If Gate0 = TIGHTENED → synthesis is prefixed with "CAUTIOUS" and position rules are tightened
  • If Gate0 = PROCEED → normal synthesis rules apply

Known Failure Modes

  • Sudden liquidity shocks not in the Black Swan score — manual override required
  • AI-3 wave module is experimental — treat as MEDIUM confidence
  • Weekend illiquidity — session module downgrades but doesn't fully neutralize noise
