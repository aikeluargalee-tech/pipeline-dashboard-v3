Breakout-Retest: The Full Setup Lifecycle

Trading Execution · 8 min read · Part of the execution cluster

The breakout-retest is one of the highest-probability trading setups: price breaks above resistance, pulls back to test the broken level, and bounces. The Pipeline Dashboard doesn't just detect the breakout — it tracks the entire lifecycle from initial break to entry signal.

The Four Stages

Stage 1: BREAKOUT_DETECTED

Price breaks above 4H resistance with volume confirmation. The dashboard requires:

  • Price above resistance in recent 15m candles
  • Taker buy ratio > 1.2 (aggressive buyers)
  • CVD > 0 (positive cumulative flow)
  • No magnet cluster within 0.5% above (would cause a sweep)
  • Minimum volume during thin hours (50 BTC per 15m candle)
  • At least 0.5x ATR of space above to the next magnet

When all conditions are met, a retest zone is created with a 48-hour expiry window.

Stage 2: RETEST_ACTIVE

Price pulls back to within 0.3% of the broken resistance level. The zone is marked as "retested." The system now watches for bounce confirmation.

Stage 3: ENTRY_SIGNAL

Price bounces back above the level by 0.3%, and the 1-hour candle closes above the level. This confirms the retest held and the level is now support. Entry signal includes:

  • Direction: LONG
  • Stop loss: 1% below the broken level
  • Target: Next resistance above, or VAH, or 2% above level
  • Confidence: HIGH if price is 1%+ above level, MEDIUM otherwise

Stage 4: Outcome Tracking

Once an entry signal fires, it's logged to signal history with WIN/LOSS/EXPIRED tracking. The signal is evaluated over 48 hours — WIN if price reaches target, LOSS if price hits stop, EXPIRED if neither within 48h.

Kill Conditions

A retest zone can fail before producing an entry signal:

  • Failed: Price closes 0.5% below the level — the breakout was a fakeout
  • Expired: 48 hours pass without a retest or bounce

Edge Case Filters

The detector includes several filters to avoid false signals:

  • Magnet sweep filter: If a liquidation magnet is within 0.5% above resistance, the breakout may be a sweep — skip
  • Thin hours filter: During 00:00-06:00 UTC, require minimum 50 BTC per 15m candle
  • Space requirement: Need at least 0.5x ATR between the broken level and the next magnet
