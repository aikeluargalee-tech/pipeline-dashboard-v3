🧱 Dashboard Framework (6 Strategic Blocks)

To maintain an objective, multi-layered view of market conditions without relying on single-metric predictions, the dashboard is organized into 6 distinct analytical blocks:

1. Market Context & Observation Classifies the current macro regime (Ranging, Trending, Cascade, etc.) using key volatility and price action models.
2. Risk & Systemic Health Monitors Tracks systemic risk parameters (Gate0, Black Swan indicators, and D2 Crash precursors) to detect market stress.
3. Market Data Layers Aggregates core metrics across layers: Macroeconomics (VIX, DXY), Structural Liquidity (Heatmaps, Support/Resistance), and Derivatives (OI, Funding Rates).

📖 How are Market States derived and what do they mean?

The Six Market States Explained

📈 TRENDING: Price is moving consistently in one direction (up or down) with strong momentum.
🔄 RANGING: Price is consolidating, fluctuating within a stable horizontal range.
💥 CASCADE: Sudden high-speed liquidation sweeps causing rapid, high-volatility price swings.
🛡️ RISK_OFF: Panic in traditional equity markets causing capital flight out of high-risk assets like BTC.
📥 DISTRIBUTION: Selling pressure near local cycle tops, representing a transitional top-out phase.
❓ UNCERTAIN: Conflicting data indicators, indicating no clear trend direction.

How the State is Mathematically Derived

Every 15 minutes, the automated pipeline computes these four objective quantitative inputs to classify the market state without human bias:

  1. Trend Filter (MA50): Measures current price deviations against the 50-period moving average.
  2. Volatility Expansion (ATR): Uses Average True Range to detect expansion (trending) or compression (ranging).
  3. Leverage Sweeps: Scans perpetual futures Open Interest (OI) and CVD to capture liquidation liquidities.
  4. Systemic TradFi Spreads: Monitors VIX (stock volatility index) and DXY (dollar strength) to spot macro panics.

🛡️ Systemic Risk Monitor

📖 Model Methodology ▼

What is the Systemic Risk Monitor?

It measures the vulnerability of the cryptocurrency market by tracking seven key stability metrics, including stablecoin peg deviations, broad equity market panic, and major currency trends.

What do the status levels mean?

Green (NORMAL) represents low systemic risk. Yellow (CAUTION) or Orange (ELEVATED) indicates moderate structural stress. Red (CRITICAL) warns of extreme market vulnerability.

What do the seven metrics monitor?

• Sentiment (Fear & Greed): The emotional state of crowd sentiment (Fear = panic; Greed = FOMO; Normal = calm).
• Equity Volatility (VIX): Traditional stock market fear index. Calm stock markets equal normal VIX.
• Stablecoin Pegs: Verifies if dollar-pegged digital coins are holding their stable $1.00 USD value.
• US Dollar (DXY): Measures global dollar strength. A surging dollar historically pressures Bitcoin.
• US 10Y Treasury: Tracks benchmark interest rates for signs of borrowing cost spikes or stress.
• Whale Activity: Observes transaction behaviors of massive holders (Dumping = selling pressure; Balanced = normal).
• Crash Signals: Real-time scan of futures leverage and order book retreat flags before sudden price drops.

⚠️ Market Instability Precursors

Real-time volatility observations: taker aggression, bid wall decay, funding stress, OI implosion, divergence.

📖 Model Methodology ▼

What are Market Instability Precursors?

These are early-warning signs that highlight when short-term conditions are becoming unstable. When multiple warning indicators fire together, it indicates an increased likelihood of a sudden, sharp price movement.

What do the individual warning metrics track?

Aggressive selling measures immediate sell volume imbalances. Support wall retreat tracks large buyers pulling orders. Leverage fees show cost spikes for holding futures. Leverage flushes monitor forced liquidations, and momentum disconnect tracks price-to-indicator deviations.

What are the Gate 0 System Safety Switches?

Gate 0 acts as a real-time safety filter. If any safety switch flags high risk, the system moves to a cautious state (like TIGHTENED or PAUSE) to highlight unstable cross-market conditions.

Dashboard Methodology

How this dashboard collects and processes data

This pipeline engine synthesizes Bitcoin (BTC) market structure by correlating broader Macroeconomic Conditions with real-time Structural Liquidity. Rather than relying on lagging technical indicators, the dashboard tracks Liquidation Magnets, Open Interest, and On-Chain Valuation to identify specific price levels where institutional capital and leveraged order flow are concentrated. This physics-based approach provides an objective framework for anticipating Bitcoin's directional volatility across different market cycles.

Data sources: Binance (price, funding, open interest), FRED (DXY, yields, M2 supply), Yahoo Finance (SPX, VIX, risk assets), Coinglass (liquidation heatmaps), and on-chain providers (MVRV Z-Score, SOPR, Puell Multiple). All data refreshes every 15 minutes. Gate0 acts as a safety filter to flag when overall market conditions are unusually risky.
