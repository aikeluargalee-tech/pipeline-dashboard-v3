Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
TL;DR: We do not directly log personal identifiers, but third-party services (such as Google AdSense) utilize cookies for serving personalized advertisements.

Third-Party Advertising & Cookies (Google AdSense)

This website utilizes third-party vendors, including Google, which use cookies to serve personalized ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may choose to opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

Data Collection

Outside of third-party advertising cookies managed by Google, the Pipeline Dashboard does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no proprietary analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or server-side logging of user activity on our infrastructure.

How the Dashboard Works

The dashboard runs entirely in your browser. When you visit, your browser fetches static JSON data files from GitHub Pages. No information about you — your IP, location, browsing history, or device — is sent to any server. The data flows one way: from our server to your browser.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address is processed by Buttondown (our newsletter provider) under their privacy policy. We do not store your email on our servers. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Contact Form

If you use the contact form, your message is processed by Formspree. We receive your name, email, and message — nothing else. We do not store this data beyond responding to your message.

Third-Party Data Sources

The dashboard displays data from third-party APIs (Binance, Yahoo Finance, FRED, Coinglass). Your browser does not contact these services directly — all data is fetched server-side and served as static JSON files. Your IP address is not shared with these services.

GitHub Pages

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard server access logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of their infrastructure. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Given our current architecture (static HTML, no server-side processing), it is unlikely we will ever collect data.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us or open an issue on GitHub.
